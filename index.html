THE STORY

In the high-stakes world of beauty, three women from completely different backgrounds have built their own empires—Ava Monet, Jade Park, and Zariah Boudreaux. But when fake beauty products flood the market, endangering customers and threatening their businesses, they discover a shadow operation led by Lilith Deveraux, a ruthless crime boss using counterfeit beauty supplies to launder money.

What starts as a business rivalry turns into an all-out war as the scammers escalate their attacks—robbing stores, setting businesses on fire, and making it clear that anyone who stands in their way won't survive.

Forced to set aside their differences, the three women form a secret alliance—the Beauty Connect—turning their businesses into a powerful underground beauty mafia. With money, power, and their entire industry at stake, they must outsmart Lilith's network, take down her empire, and reclaim their control over the beauty game.

THE ENEMY

LILITH DEVERAUX

THE CRIMINAL MASTERMIND

Behind the glamorous facade of the beauty industry lurks a shadow operation led by Lilith Deveraux, a ruthless crime boss using counterfeit beauty supplies to launder money on a global scale.

With an international network of suppliers and distributors, Lilith's operation threatens not just businesses, but lives—her fake products containing dangerous chemicals that have already harmed countless unsuspecting customers.

As her empire grows, so does her boldness—and she won't let three women stand in her way, no matter how powerful they've become.
